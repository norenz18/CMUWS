CMU

The City of Malabon University,
Home of the City Scholars envisions to become an institution that will bring about a higher standard of living and quality of life through academic excellence research and community linkages.

Vision

The City of Malabon University home of the "City Scholars", is envisioned to be the center of excellence which shall bridge the learners to attain a higher standard of a living by providing a highly cognitive and value-laden education.

Mission

The City of Malabon University shall develop holistic learners competent workforce,
and guide them to their journey to excellence; encourage involvement of the stakegolders to create a better synergy with the community.
